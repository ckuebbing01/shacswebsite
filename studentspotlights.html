Student Clubs

Hack Club

The Hack club at SHA is an all-girls, Louisville-based high school club as a part of the international Hack Club program. We are focused on learning to code, developing design skills, and having fun! The Hack Club meets weekly. Workshops include creating your own website, designing in Figma, creating meme generators, creating 3D designs and more! Once a year, the SHA Hack Club hosts a Hack Night with the Saint Xavier boys Hack Club.

VEX Robotics

The VEX Robotics team at SHA meets weekly. Teams of students are tasked with designing and building a robot to play against other teams in a game-based engineering and coding challenge.
