Why Computer Science?

Computer programming is creative. It allows us to change the world!

Computer Science Concentration at SHA

The Computer Science Concentration is a concentration that begins freshmen year. Courses in computer science are taken each year. No prior experience is necessary. Students should sign up for the computer science concentration if they are genuinely interested in learning about:

  • Technology
  • Design
  • Building apps
  • Coding
  • Making games
  • Making websites

Why Take Computer Science at SHA?

  • It’s not stressful.
  • There's rarely homework.
  • It’s collaborative.
  • It’s applicable to life and other classes.
  • It really puts technology and the time it takes to create technology into perspective.
  • Learning about the design aspect is fun.
  • It’s a supportive learning environment.
  • You can earn college credit through the two AP courses.
